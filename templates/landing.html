Welcome to Papaya.

Papaya is a web app that analyzes your Google Calendar. We display graphical visualizations of your calendar data to provide relevant insights into how you spend your time.

We welcome existing and aspiring "Quantified Self" enthusiasts: How much time do you spend on your top five hobbies? What percentage of your time do you actually track? How many meetings do you go to throughout the week?

Connect your Google Calendar to discover your temporal sense of self.

Privacy Policy: We utilize the Google Calendar API to collect your calendar information. We will not share or sell your data.
